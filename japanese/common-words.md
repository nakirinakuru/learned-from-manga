Japanese Common Words Learned from Manga
===========================================

## Pronouns

* 僕ら

  ぼくら
  
  (n) (male) we

* 俺

  おれ
  
  (pn,adj-no) (male) (sounds rough or arrogant) I, me

* 彼女

  かのじょ

  (pn,adj-no) (1) she, her

## Schools

* 中学

  ちゅうがく
  
  (n) (abbr) middle school, junior high school
